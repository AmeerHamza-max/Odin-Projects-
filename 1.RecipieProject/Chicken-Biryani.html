Chicken Biryani

Home

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant South Asian rice dish made by layering aromatic basmati rice with spiced chicken curry. Known for its rich flavors and beautiful golden hue, it’s a festive favorite in Pakistan, India, and beyond.

Indegredients

    For chicken masala:
  • Chicken – 1 kg (cut into medium pieces)
  • Onions – 3 large (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes – 3 medium (chopped)
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 4 (whole or slit)
  • Cooking oil – ½ cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Red chili powder – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp (store-bought or homemade)
  • Fresh coriander – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Fresh mint – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • For rice:
  • Basmati rice – 4 cups (soaked for 30 mins)
  • Salt – 2 tbsp
  • Whole spices – 1 cinnamon stick, 4 cardmoms, 4 cloves, 2 bay leaves

Instruction

  • Cook chicken masala
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, fry for 1 min.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  • Heat oil in a pot, fry onions until golden.
  • Add salt, chili powder, turmeric, biryani masala, and tomatoes. Cook until oil separates.
  • Stir in yogurt, green chilies, coriander, mint, and lemon juice. Cook for 5 mins.
  • Boil rice
  • In a large pot, boil water with salt and whole spices.
  • Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  • Layer biryani
  • In a large pot, layer half the rice, then all the chicken masala, then the remaining rice.
  • Sprinkle some coriander, mint, and a few drops of yellow food color (optional).
  • Steam (Dum)
  • Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20–25 mins.
  • Gently mix before serving.
